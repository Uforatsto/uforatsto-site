About Us

UFORATSTO INC is a New York–based trading and logistics company. We operate a modern 3PL prep-center that handles warehousing, labeling, and order fulfillment for e-commerce sellers; provide representative-office services for foreign businesses entering the U.S. market; and import a broad range of products — including building materials and food products.

Services

  • 3PL & Prep-Center (Amazon FBA/FBM, Walmart WFS/Seller Fulfilled, DTC)
  • Representative-office services in the USA
  • Import of products, building materials & food
  • Wholesale distribution
  • Last-mile delivery across the USA
  • Private-label sourcing
